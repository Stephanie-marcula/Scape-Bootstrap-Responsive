About us

Welcome to Scape, the ultimate hangout for skaters of all ages and levels! Our mission is to promote the spirit of skateboarding through an inclusive and vibrant environment where adrenaline, creativity and community meet.

  • Complete Store: From custom skateboards to essential accessories, we have everything you need.
  • Skate Park: An innovative park designed by skaters for skaters, with challenging obstacles and areas for all levels.
  • Events and Workshops: We organize exciting competitions, workshops with renowned professionals.

Come live the Scape experience: Slide, learn, connect and be part of a unique culture that celebrates skateboarding in all its forms.
